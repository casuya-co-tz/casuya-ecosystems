Casuya is not a single product — it is an ecosystem of purpose-built packages that compose into a complete education platform. Each package is independently versioned, tested, and reusable, wired together through shared contracts, schemas, and a unified API gateway.

The 24-Package Ecosystem

Everything that powers Casuya

Grouped into six architectural layers — from low-level utilities to user-facing experiences.

Core & Runtime

📚casuya-platform

Main FastAPI backend: auth, lessons, quizzes, games, progress, search, uploads.
📨casuya-api

REST / GraphQL / WebSocket gateway with caching, contracts & monitoring.
🧪casuya-core

Lesson packaging, compiling, signing, validation and versioning.
⏱️casuya-runtime

Sandboxed lesson runtime with HTML/CSS/Canvas/Media renderers & policies.
📋casuya-common

Shared utilities: strings, arrays, validators, crypto, dates, logging.

Content & Intelligence

📝casuya-content

Content repository, taxonomies, categories, tags & publishing workflows.
🖊️casuya-editor

Rich lesson editor for authoring interactive, media-rich content.
🤖casuya-ai

AI tutoring, recommendations, quiz generation, summaries & translation.
🔍casuya-search

Full-text indexing, ranking, autocomplete & faceted filtering.
📊casuya-analytics

ETL pipelines, trend analysis, anomaly detection & predictions.
📚casuya-exams

Question bank, exam builder, auto-grading, certificates & reports.
🧮casuya-math

Math, science equation & graph rendering for interactive lessons.
✏️casuya-blackboard

Interactive digital blackboard with stylus input & handwriting recognition.

Access & Engagement

🔑casuya-auth

Auth, authorization, MFA, SSO (Google/Microsoft/OAuth) & sessions.
🌉casuya-bridge

Offline-first sync engine: IndexedDB, conflict resolution & encryption.
🔔casuya-notifications

Multi-channel (in-app/email/SMS/push) templates, scheduling & retries.
💳casuya-payments

Payment engine, subscriptions, refunds, invoices & reconciliation.

Infrastructure & Tooling

🎞️casuya-media

Image/video/audio transcoding, thumbnails, compression & streaming.
🚀casuya-deployment

Infrastructure-as-code, environments & release automation.
⚙️casuya-orchestrator

Workflow engine, blue-green/canary pipelines, monitoring & governance.
🛠️casuya-devtools

CLI with repo generators, architecture validation & automation.
📦casuya-sdk

Typed client SDK for consuming the Casuya APIs with ease.
📚casuya-docs

Documentation site, guides & API references for the ecosystem.

Frontend

🎨casuya-design-system

30+ React components, hooks, design tokens & light/dark/high-contrast themes.

How It Fits Together

One request, many packages

1 · Client

Student, Teacher & Admin dashboards served from casuya-platform, styled by casuya-design-system.

→
2 · Gateway

casuya-api routes, caches & authenticates via casuya-auth before reaching services.

→
3 · Services

Lessons, quizzes, AI, payments, analytics & search operate as independent packages.

Offline & Sync

casuya-bridge keeps learning working without a connection and syncs when back online.

Content

casuya-core packages & signs lessons; casuya-runtime renders them safely in the browser.

Operations

casuya-orchestrator + casuya-deployment ship & monitor every package continuously.
